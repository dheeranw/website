Curriculum Vitae

I am a third-year mathematics undergraduate at the University of Illinois studying algebra, topology, and quantum information. Recently, monoidal categories (e.g., \(\textsf{Hilb}, \textsf{Rep}_G, \textsf{Bord}_d\)) and categorical quantum things have my attention.

During the summer of 2025, I became a MathQuantum Fellow at the University of Maryland, College Park. In August, I was at the Summer School on Geometry, hosted by the Utrecht Geometry Centre at Universiteit Utrecht.

Expository

  • Two-dimensional dagger TQFTs are pretty simple
    dagger categories \(\mathsf{Bord}_2\) and \(\mathsf{FdHilb}\), dagger Frobenius algebras, H*-algebras, semisimplicity, Ambrose's theorem, Durhuus-Jonsson classification
  • Bredon cohomology for Smith theory
    \(G\)-spaces, \(G\)-CW complexes, homotopy categories, \(\mathcal{O}_G\), coefficient systems, cellular Bredon cohomology, augmentation ideal, Smith theory
  • What is a TQFT? A symmetric monoidal functor.
    monoidal categories, bordism class category braidings, (braided) monoidal functors, monoidal natural transformations, \(\mathsf{TQFT}_2 \simeq \mathsf{CFrob}\), daggers
  • Commutant of the Clifford group
    Clifford twirling, \(\operatorname{Com}(\mathcal{C}_n^{\otimes k})\), graph-based Pauli monomials, Clifford-Weingarten calculus, de Finetti theorem, robust Hudson theorem
  • An introduction to sheaf cohomology
    abelian categories, (pre)sheaves, functor categories, derived functors, sheaf cohomology, Čech cohomology, first cousin problem
