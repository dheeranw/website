Curriculum Vitae

Notes

I take my notes live in \(\LaTeX\), largely to motivate myself to attend lecture. These, rather reliably, contain mistakes or missing proofs, depending upon my attentiveness on a given day.
  • Math 527: Homotopy Theory (Charles Rezk; in progress)
    nerves, \(\infty\)-categories, fibrations, anodyne, slices, Joyal lifting, \(\mathsf{Cat}_{\infty}\), \(\pi_n : \mathsf{Kan}_* \to \mathsf{Set}_*\), presentability, stability, spectra
  • Math 525: Algebraic Topology I (James Pascaleff; in progress)
    homotopy, \(\pi_1(X,x_0)\), covering spaces, lifting, CW complexes, \(\Delta\)-complexes, Galois covers, singular and cellular homology, Eilenberg-Steenrod axioms
  • An Introduction to Sheaf Cohomology
    abelian categories, (pre)sheaves, functor categories, derived functors, sheaf cohomology, Čech cohomology, first cousin problem
  • Math 501: Abstract Algebra II (Igor Mineyev; in progress)
    categories, (co)limits, \(\operatorname{Hom}\) and \(\otimes_R\), adjunctions, (co)homology, \(\operatorname{Tor}_n^R\) and \(\operatorname{Ext}^n_R\), spectral sequences, multilinear algebra, representations
  • MATH 500: Abstract Algebra I (Charles Rezk)
    free groups, \(\textsf{Grp}\), actions, \(\textsf{Set}_G\), group rings, domains, \(\textsf{Mod}_R\), coproducts, semisimple rings, modules over PIDs, fields, Galois correspondence
  • MATH 535: General Topology (Igor Mineyev)
    homeomorphisms, invariants, \(\ell^p(\mathbb{N}, \mathbb{R})\), separability, embeddings, \(Y^X\), \(\mathcal{B}(X,Y) \), \(\mathcal{C}(X,Y)\), completions, \(\pi_1: \mathsf{Top}_{*} \to \mathsf{Grp}\)
  • ECE 498: Quantum Information Processing Theory (Eric Chitambar)
    state spaces, tensor products, \(\operatorname{SU}(2) \simeq \operatorname{SO}(3)\), CPTP channels, Pauli twirling, POVMs, LOCC, teleportation, nonlocality
  • MATH 418: Intro to Abstract Algebra II (Andy Hardt)
    domains, \(R[x]\) irreducibility, fields and towers, \(\operatorname{Gal}(\mathbb{K}/\mathbb{L})\), \(\mathbb{C}\mathrm{P}^n\), projective and affine Nullstellensatz
  • MATH 424: Honors Real Analysis (Eugene Lerman)
    topology, metric spaces, differentiation, integration, interchange of limit operations, Lebesgue measure and integration, \(L^1(E,d \mu)\)
